History Of Itihas

A history library is a special type of library that focuses on preserving and providing information about past events, cultures, and civilizations. It contains valuable resources such as old manuscripts, historical documents, books, maps, and research papers that help people understand history in detail. Students, researchers, and historians use history libraries to study important topics like the Indian Independence Movement through authentic sources. These libraries also play a key role in protecting cultural heritage and ensuring that knowledge of the past is passed on to future generations, making them an important part of education and research.

Mission

To preserve historical knowledge and provide authentic resources for students, researchers, and history enthusiasts.

Vision

To become a leading history library that promotes learning, research, and cultural awareness.

History

Our history library preserves valuable records and documents, helping people explore important past events and heritage.
